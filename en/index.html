Learn more about SKIN'Cast™

    Are you looking to offer your patients an optimal prosthetic fit but current tools don't let you achieve the required precision?


    Thanks to SKIN'Cast™, benefit from precise, objective analysis tools to reduce the number of iterations required for prosthetic adjustments. By collecting essential data from the very first appointment, we help you adjust and align prostheses optimally, perfectly suited to your patients’ daily needs.

    Reduced correction iterations

    Thanks to precise, real-time measurements, SKIN'Cast reduces the number of adjustment sessions needed, saving time and resources for both professionals and patients.

    Active support for complex cases

    SKIN'Cast helps solve the most challenging cases by providing objective data, enabling a constructive dialogue between the professional and the patient to find the best solution together.

    Inclusive follow-up for the patient

    SKIN'Cast facilitates collaboration among all healthcare professionals involved in the patient’s journey, enriching the medical record to ensure optimal follow-up and future device durability.

    Dynamic analysis and advanced biomarkers

    The SKIN'Cast interface provides real-time visualization of applied pressures, coupled with biomarkers generated by our machine learning (AI) models, enabling optimal adjustments and patient comfort.
