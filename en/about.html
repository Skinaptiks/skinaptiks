About SKINAPTIKS

Touch the essence with meaningful technologies

Est 2014

At the Heart of Tactile Innovation

It all began with a clear ambition: to push the limits of what technology can offer in the medical field. SKINAPTIKS was born from the desire to turn technological expertise into concrete solutions capable of meeting the complex challenges faced by healthcare professionals.

Initially, our team focused on a pioneering research project: equipping robots with fine tactile perception through an electronic skin. This project, carried out by Cyril Bounakoff during his doctoral research, aimed to enable machines to perform dexterous tasks and interact more naturally with humans. Guided by renowned mentors such as François Michaud for robotics, Jacques Beauvais for micro-technology, Jonathan Genest for innovation, and Vincent Hayward for haptics, we quickly realized that this innovation could have an impact far beyond robotics.

Est 2022

An Impact that Makes Sense, a Touch that Transforms

Hence, SKINAPTIKS was established with a broader mission: adapting this cutting-edge technology to improve the daily lives of amputees and facilitate the work of healthcare professionals.

Our Strategy is Built on Three Fundamental Pillars

Integration and Expertise

We master key areas such as haptics, robotics, and medical applications, supported by top experts to develop cutting-edge technological solutions.

Technology and Innovation

Our know-how spans micro- and nanotechnologies, artificial intelligence, embedded systems, electronic board design, and software tools, enabling us to create advanced and innovative systems.

Passion, Determination, and Values

Our core motivation is to contribute to society by developing technologies that improve patients’ lives and facilitate the work of healthcare professionals.

Today

From the Lab to the Hands of Professionals

Field trials have begun, marking a key milestone in the evolution of SKINAPTIKS. With a strong network of partners and experts, we are accelerating the development and future commercialization of SKIN'Cast™. This advancement brings our technology even closer to the healthcare professionals and patients who will benefit from it.
