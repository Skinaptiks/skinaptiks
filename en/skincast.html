About SKIN'Cast

Are you aiming to provide your patients with an optimal prosthetic fit, but current tools don’t allow you to achieve the necessary precision?

With SKIN'Cast™, benefit from precise, objective analysis tools to reduce the number of iterations needed for prosthetic adjustments. By collecting essential data starting with the first appointment, we help you optimally adjust and align prostheses, perfectly tailored to your patients’ everyday needs.

Reduced Correction Iterations

Thanks to precise, real-time measurements, SKIN'Cast reduces the number of adjustment sessions required, saving time and resources for both professionals and patients.

Active Support for Complex Cases

SKIN'Cast helps solve the toughest cases by providing objective data, enabling constructive dialogue between the professional and the patient to find the best solution together.

Inclusive Discussion and Follow-Up for the Patient

SKIN'Cast facilitates collaboration among all healthcare professionals involved in the patient’s journey, enhancing the medical record to ensure optimal follow-up and the future durability of the device.

Dynamic Analysis and Advanced Biomarkers

The SKIN'Cast interface provides real-time visualization of applied pressures, along with biomarkers generated by our machine learning (AI) models, enabling optimal adjustments and greater patient comfort.
